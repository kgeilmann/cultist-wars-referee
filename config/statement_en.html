The Goal

The aim of this game is to gain as many followers as possible. You win if your opponents number of units drops to 0 or if you have more units than your enemy by the last round.

  Rules

Your program receives characters.
Your actions have an effect.

The game zone works as follows:
  • There are coordinates.

The units work as follows:
  • They move around.
You lose if:
  • You mess up.
  • You do not supply a valid sequence of actions.
